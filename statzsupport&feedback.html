Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset by going to Profile > Settings (gear button on the top right corner).

How is my data stored?

All your data is securely stored in the cloud using Firebase, with encryption in transit and at rest.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can delete your account from the app settings at any time. All associated data will be permanently removed.
